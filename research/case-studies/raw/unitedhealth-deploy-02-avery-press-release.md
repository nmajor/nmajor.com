"UnitedHealthcare today introduced Avery, a generative AI companion that creates a simpler, more coordinated health care experience for members while empowering customer advocates to more efficiently respond to members."

"Avery brings together information from multiple sources about benefits, providers and claims into one simple, unified experience..."

"Designed to support the entire care journey, Avery can call network primary care providers to schedule appointments on a member's behalf, striving to reduce friction and administrative burden."

## Analysis note (honesty-critical)
- Avery is EXPLICITLY "generative AI," stated by UnitedHealthcare itself.
- Scope is member customer-service: coverage/benefit Q&A, scheduling, cost estimates, claim approval STATUS (not adjudication decisions), EOB explanations, provider search.
- "Avery can call network primary care providers to schedule appointments" = the "bots calling doctors" headline; it is appointment scheduling, NOT calling to pursue denials or authorizations.
- Explicitly framed as "human expertise guiding output" — human-in-the-loop.
- Avery does NOT adjudicate or deny claims per this release.
