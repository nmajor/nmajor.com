# PYMNTS (reporting Bloomberg, Oct 2025) — "Hospital Group Says UnitedHealth AI System Reduces Claims Denials"
https://www.pymnts.com/healthcare/2025/hospital-group-says-unitedhealth-ai-system-reduces-claims-denials/
Fetched 2026-07-07.

Extracted relevant text (verbatim):
"The Optum Real system, which is designed to streamline medical claims processing, has reduced denials by flagging claims that need more documentation before they are submitted, Bloomberg reported Tuesday (Oct. 21)."
"Dave Ingham, chief digital and information officer at Allina Health, told Bloomberg that the AI system avoids the disappointment of denials and reduces paperwork."
"The Optum Real system was developed by UnitedHealth's Optum Insight unit and connects with UnitedHealth's insurance division, UnitedHealthcare, according to the report."
"The company aims to add more insurance and providers to the system by providing them with lower transaction costs, Optum Insight CEO Sandeep Dadlani told Bloomberg."
"Optum Insight does not charge a fee for those companies to connect to the system's core functions... Instead, it plans to make money by helping them digitize their information and by providing companies with advanced analytics and other premium features."

## Analysis note (honesty-critical)
- Independent provider-side corroboration (Allina Health CDIO Dave Ingham) that Optum Real, as used, REDUCES denials by flagging missing documentation pre-submission — the opposite direction from "AI denying claims."
- This is the key counter-fact for honesty: the CURRENT named claims/adjudication AI (Optum Real) is reported to reduce denials, not generate them, and is not described as generative/LLM.
