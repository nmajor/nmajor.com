## SOURCE: Digital Applied — "AI Lead Magnets: 7 Templates That Capture Emails"

AI Readiness Checklist = "the single highest-converting lead magnet format for AI agencies."
- Mechanism: prospect scores themselves across 25 dimensions, gets a concrete readiness rating, immediately understands gaps.
- Template: 25-point scorecard across five categories (data infrastructure, team skills, process maturity, technology stack, strategic alignment), each scored 0-2, totaling 50 points.
- Score-based recommendations: 0-16 -> "Focus on data infrastructure and team training before investing in AI tools"; 34-50 -> "begin pilot projects with measurable ROI targets."

Conversion performance (VERBATIM numbers — FLAG: single vendor blog, treat as directional):
- AI readiness checklists: "30-40% opt-in rates" on dedicated landing pages; "15-20%" inline within blog posts.
- Generic "Subscribe for AI insights" opt-in boxes: "1-3%" (from related search result, ScoreApp/usafinancial corroboration).
- ROI calculators: convert because they answer "What will this cost me versus what will I gain?"
- Mini-courses: "Five touchpoints over five days builds more trust than one PDF ever can. Subscribers who complete a 5-day mini-course convert to paid consultations at 2-3x the rate."
- Audit reports: convert at "15-25%" because audience self-identified as high-intent.

Human expertise still essential: AI-generated content + "10-15 minutes" of expert review per asset.

Why interactive assessments work (VERBATIM-ish): "Gap awareness is what drives prospects to book a consultation. Interactive tools like scorecards and self-assessments are effective because they insert the prospect into the content, and seeing a score builds awareness and urgency."

ON-BRAND NOTE: an AI readiness assessment fits the anti-hype thesis perfectly — it's diagnostic, honest about where the buyer is NOT ready, and surfaces real gaps rather than pushing tools. Then auto-email -> deeper insights / book free consultation.
