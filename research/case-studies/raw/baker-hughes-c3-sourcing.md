Concrete numbers (vendor-reported):
- 900,000 SKUs supported by C3 AI Sourcing Optimization.
- 800+ sites covered at full-scale implementation.
- "Millions" in economic benefit realized in calendar year 2025 (exact figure NOT specified).
- 57,000 employees; 120+ countries; $7B+ quarterly revenue.
- 30M+ records from 5 enterprise data sources integrated.
- 200+ active users of the system.
- 5-7% acceptance rate on identified cost-saving opportunities.

What the system does:
- Presents sourcing opportunities with total landed cost breakdowns, similar-part rationale,
  and index-based pricing signals.
- Real-time market monitoring via live tariff feeds; iOS/Android mobile access.
- Human-in-the-loop: buyers accept or reject recommendations; algorithms self-improve on feedback.

Challenge addressed:
- Fragmented data scattered across disconnected systems; manual spreadsheet reconciliation.

Timeline:
- Implementation evolved from a 3-site pilot to 10 sites, then enterprise-wide. Began in 2019.
