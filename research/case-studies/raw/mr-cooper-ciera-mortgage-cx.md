# Raw source capture — Mr. Cooper AI in mortgage servicing (fetched 2026-07-07)

## Google Cloud Blog — "Assembling a team of AI agents to handle complex mortgage questions at Mr. Cooper"
https://cloud.google.com/blog/topics/financial-services/assembling-a-team-of-ai-agents-to-handle-complex-mortgage-questions-at-mr-cooper
- Mr. Cooper = American home-loan servicer.
- Built CIERA (Coaching Intelligent Education & Resource Agent): a multi-agent "AI agent workforce" on Google Cloud Vertex AI supporting call-center agents; handles repetitive and complex tasks so human agents focus on what tech can't.
- Human-in-the-loop; partnered with Google Cloud Consulting.
- Governance: developed two concepts — an "agentic pulse" and human oversight — as an evaluation/governance framework for a regulated industry ("trust and accuracy are non-negotiable").

## National Mortgage Professional — "Mr. Cooper Bets Big on AI, Expands Tech for Better CX"
https://nationalmortgageprofessional.com/news/mr-cooper-bets-big-ai-expands-tech-better-cx
- On an earnings call, president Mike Weinbach described "Agent IQ," an agentic framework that assists call-center members by analyzing conversations in real time; detects customer intent and sentiment trends.
- Agent IQ trained on millions of customer calls; can "fetch" relevant info to agents' fingertips; generates transcript + call summary.
- CEO Jay Bray: closed acquisition of Flagstar's mortgage-banking operations, onboarded 1.1M customers ("by far the largest acquisition in our history"). In January sold Flagstar's TPO/broker platform to A&D Mortgage.
- (Separately reported: Mr. Cooper being acquired by / rebranding under Rocket Mortgage — per Mr. Cooper help-center notices.)

## gspann.com — "AI Agent Governance in CX: ROI vs. 74% Rollback Rate" (Jun 18, 2026)
https://www.gspann.com/insights/blog/ai-agent-governance-customer-experience
- "Mr. Cooper, a US mortgage servicer handling 500,000 monthly calls, deployed AI assistance across its contact center and unlocked 28,000 hours of agent capacity annually." (Secondary source; the 28,000-hours and 500k-calls figures appear here, not in the Google Cloud primary.)
