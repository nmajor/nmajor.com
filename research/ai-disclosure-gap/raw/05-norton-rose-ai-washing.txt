"In 2024, the SEC took several enforcement actions against companies that allegedly made false or misleading statements regarding their AI capabilities."
Charged two investment advisory firms (Delphia, Global Predictions) March 2024 for misrepresenting AI use.
January 2025: settlement with a consumer-facing tech company (Presto Automation - first AI-washing action vs a public company).
Gensler: "false claims about AI hurt investors and undermine market integrity."
SUPPLEMENTARY (Corporate Compliance Insights / Cornerstone): securities class actions alleging AI misrepresentation rose ~100% between 2023 and 2024.
