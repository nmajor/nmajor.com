# Named practitioners: Ruben Hassid, Sahil Bloom, Dakota Robertson

Accessed: 2026-06-19

## Ruben Hassid (ruben-hassid) — AI niche, most relevant peer
Sources: ruben.substack.com/p/from-49-to-10000-followers-in-17, theaireport.ai, synthesia case study, viralbrain.ai
- Profile: grew from 8,000 (pre-ChatGPT) to 770,000 in 3 years; replicated across 5 LinkedIn accounts for combined 1.56M followers organically. Founder of EasyGen. 60M+ video views via personal AI avatar (Synthesia).
- "10,000 followers in 17 days" tactics (from 49 start):
  - Profile pic: "Smile way more than you think." "Own a color. Instantly recognizable." "Zoom until your entire face is the only thing visible."
  - Headline/mission: be instantly clear on value prop. His: "Master AI before it masters you."
  - Audience: EXTREME specificity — "You're not making content for millions. You're creating content for exactly ONE person." Build detailed ICP.
  - Pain-point research: use Reddit (he uses a "Red Searcher" GPT) to find viral pain points in your target community, then make content addressing them.
  - Authenticity: avoid AI-sounding patterns; he dislikes the overused "This is not about X. This is about Y" because it screams AI-generated.
- Key philosophy: "What's left after AI is taste and consistency." Use AI to enhance, not replace, human creativity.
- NOTE: Section 2 ("secrets to +1,000 followers/day") was paywalled.

## Sahil Bloom (sahilbloom) — finance/growth, grew 500 -> 75,000 in 1 yr, then 700K+
Sources: icon.com/skio interview, levelingup newsletter, growthinreverse
- "You can't fake content based on what you think the market wants from you. Authenticity is at the core of any successful strategy."
- Growth NOT algorithm-driven: focused on authentic stories/experiences. "Live the experiences, face the challenges, and then talk about them."
- CONSISTENCY: never missed a week with newsletter or social — foundation of long-term audience.
- Constructs "zero-to-one acquisition funnels"; growth tactics that compound with each repetition.

## Dakota Robertson (dakotarobertson) — ghostwriter/content
- Made a video for Sahil Bloom that hit 4M views.
- Focus: monetizing (not just growing) an audience; content marketing for Twitter + LinkedIn.
- Emphasizes authenticity, consistency, strategic audience development.

## ON-BRAND vs AVOID
- HIGHLY ON-BRAND: Ruben's "taste + consistency," ICP-of-one, real Reddit pain points, anti-AI-slop voice (perfect for an anti-hype AI expert). Sahil's "live it then talk about it," authenticity, never-miss-a-week consistency.
- AVOID/CAUTION: Ruben's "10k in 17 days" framing is itself a bit hype-y; treat the underlying tactics (profile, ICP, pain-points) as the signal, ignore the velocity promise.
