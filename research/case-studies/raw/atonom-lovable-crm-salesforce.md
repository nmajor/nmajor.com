Company: Atonom (sells AI "cloud employees" for sales dev, support, and ops workflows).
Team size ~25-30 people.

What happened:
- Replaced a $40,000/year Salesforce contract (25-30 seats) with a custom CRM built on
  Lovable's AI app-building platform.
- New annual cost: ~$1,200 including hosting. Also eliminated the need for a dedicated
  CRM administrator role.
- Built by Jason (Head of Finance and Legal) - working prototype "within hours."
- CRM does: lead capture, pipeline tracking, account/opportunity creation, ARR/MRR
  tracking, dashboards, weekly sales meeting reports. Integrated with Atonom's own AI
  sales agent "Zoey" (auto-triggers outreach on form submit).
- Team then built more internal tools on Lovable (finance dashboards, revenue modeling, forecasting).
- Rejected HubSpot and Pipedrive as similarly complex/costly for their scale.

Quote: CRO Gabe Larsen - "It was 40 grand a year for 25 or 30 people. We were just like,
this is crazy for the functionality we need."

PROVENANCE CAVEAT (important): the original source of this story is Lovable's own blog
post (Lovable is the vendor whose platform was used). Analytics India, TechGig, and
Fraction all re-report the Lovable case study. So the $40k -> $1,200 figure is
vendor-promoted customer marketing, not independently audited. The company and named
executives are real and consistently reported, but the numbers trace to one vendor-authored
source.
