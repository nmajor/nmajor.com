## Analysis note (honesty-critical)
- Optum Real is described by Optum itself as "AI-first," "human-centered AI and automation," and "ambient intelligence." The press release does NOT call it generative AI or an LLM.
- Its described function is real-time data exchange, surfacing/analyzing contract rules, and intercepting "known issues" at claim submission — i.e., rules/analysis automation over adjudication, NOT a described LLM decision-maker denying claims.
- Framed positively (reduce avoidable denials), not as a denial engine.
