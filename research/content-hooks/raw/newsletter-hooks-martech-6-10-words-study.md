# MarTech — Study: 6 to 10 Word Subject Lines Deliver Highest Open Rates (Retention Science)

Source: https://martech.org/study-email-marketing-subject-lines-6-10-words-deliver-highest-open-rates/
Accessed: 2026-06-19

## Study Details
- Researcher: Retention Science
- Sample: 260+ million emails across 540 retail email marketing campaigns

## Word Count vs Open Rate
- 6-10 words: 21% open rate (highest)
- 5 or fewer words: 16% open rate
- 11-15 words: 14% open rate
- Note: "most smartphones only display five or six words of a subject line," making brevity critical for mobile (35% of emails studied opened on mobile).

## Personalization
- Subject lines with recipient's first name "lifted open rates by 2.6 percent."

## Song Lyrics & Movie Titles
- Subject lines with song lyrics/movie titles: 26% open rate vs "more traditional subject lines" at 16%.
- CEO: "Movie and music references can be targeted to certain customer segments or encapsulate a promotional theme, making them a creative way to hyper-target and capture attention."

## ANALYST NOTE
6-10 word guidance is solid and credible-friendly. NOTE conflict with other sources: Campaign Monitor/VerticalResponse say 3-5 words / under 50 chars. Reconcile as: short overall (under ~50-60 chars), 6-10 words is a sweet spot, front-load the meaning because mobile truncates to ~5-6 words.
