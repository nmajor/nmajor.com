## Assessment (CONFIRMED)
Most current confirmed count: **24 states plus the District of Columbia had adopted the NAIC
Model Bulletin as of April 1, 2026**, per the NAIC's own official implementation map. This
updates/corrects the older "~24 states as of March 2025" (Hawaii is the visible net addition
since then; count of *states* is still 24 in the map's "Adopted" column, now +DC and with newer
dates). Nature of the instrument: it is a **model bulletin (soft-law guidance)**, not a model law
— it tells insurers existing unfair-trade-practice/market-conduct law applies to AI decisions and
requires an AI governance program + vendor oversight. It does NOT itself mandate a human
decision-maker on every denial. Good for "regulators expect AI governance," weaker for
"regulator requires a human in the loop."
