## What they did
Bradesco applied AI to credit modeling, the core of its banking activity, rather than the usual
customer-service or coding use cases. Under a two-year strategic plan, the credit unit was given a
dedicated AI team that uses tools including those from Brazilian AI firm Kunumi to improve credit
processes. Reported by André Duarte Oliveira, a member of Bradesco's executive committee and head
of credit, in an interview with Valor.

## The numbers
- At least R$250 million in estimated impact in the credit segment alone (bank-reported, via Valor).
- Headcount of the dedicated AI team, model types, before/after default or approval rates: unknown.
