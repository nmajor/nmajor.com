NAIC Model Bulletin requires each insurer to adopt/implement/maintain a written AI Systems (AIS) Program: governance, documented policies, senior management oversight, risk management/internal controls proportional to potential consumer harm, testing/validation for bias & unfair discrimination with documented results, and third-party vendor oversight. Adopted by 24+ jurisdictions.
Colorado SB 205 (Colorado AI Act) — first US state comprehensive AI law; requires developers/deployers of high-risk AI to protect consumers from algorithmic discrimination (governance + documentation). C.R.S. 10-3-1104.9 = Colorado insurance-specific governance & risk-management framework for AI/external consumer data.
