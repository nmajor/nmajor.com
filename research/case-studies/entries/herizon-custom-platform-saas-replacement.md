## What they did
Mari Luukkainen runs Herizon, a talent-matching program for immigrant women in Finland.
She writes that the program was paying close to 3,000 EUR a month across eight separate
SaaS tools just to run basic operations (email, contacts, community, applications, and a
few automations) and replaced that stack with a single custom platform.

## The numbers
- Before: ~3,000 EUR/month across eight tools: Customer.io (behavioral email), Mailchimp
  (newsletters), Ghost (publishing), Mighty Networks (community), Tally (intake forms),
  Zapier (glue), Buffer (social scheduling), and a CRM that "didn't really fit."
- After: one custom platform. (The exact post-migration monthly cost was not captured in
  the source text available to us; see caveat.)
- Cost / spend: stated motivation was eliminating ~3,000 EUR/month of overlapping SaaS.
- Time / headcount / volume: unknown.
