## moreinmedia.com "LinkedIn Benchmarks 2026: 5 Experts" (cites Socialinsider data)
Source: https://www.moreinmedia.com/post/linkedin-benchmarks-2026-5-experts-reveal-what-s-actually-working-and-what-to-drop-now

- Native Documents (Carousel/PDF): 7.00% (+14% YoY)
- Multi-Image Post: 6.45%
- Video: 6.00% (+7% YoY)

Expert recs (DO):
- Melonie Dodaro: post infographics more in 2026.
- Miruna Vocheci (Socialinsider): increase carousels (native documents).
- Beth Granger: test LinkedIn Newsletters as a primary format. "Bookmark 5 clients, 5 prospects, 5 referral partners — and go to their content directly."
- Kristian Downer: "find posts from people I admire that aren't getting much engagement, and leave a genuinely insightful comment." Also: thought-leader ads to amplify best organic posts.
- Miruna: lighthearted/funny content + repurposed clips from live sessions outperformed other video.

Expert recs (DROP):
- Melonie: Video (for most brands/personalities) is overrated currently.
- Beth: avoid using AI to create ALL your content.
- Kristian: LinkedIn Premium Company Pages lack value.

## authoredup / postunreel format data (3M+ posts Mar 2025-Feb 2026)
- Documents (PDF carousels): 6.60% avg engagement, highest. Generate 39% more reach + 30% more engagement than average post. Top-5% profiles: 1.72x reach, 1.63x engagement. Only 4.88% of profiles post documents regularly (underutilized).
- Video: reach -36% YoY; "video still works for long-form (3+ min), not short clips." (Conflicts somewhat with Dataslayer's "30-90s video at 5.6%" — note the disagreement.)
- Polls: 1.78x reach but only 0.37x engagement = "a reach trap, not a growth play." (Other source: polls avg 4.40%, doubled since 2023.)

## Pods + link penalty (TRUTH vs MYTH)
Sources: connectsafely.ai pods crackdown, gromming.com external-links, dataslayer
- Engagement pods: REAL penalty. Reach restrictions, shadowbans, suspensions; some 97% reach drops overnight. Detection ~97% accuracy in 2026. LinkedIn's stated goal: make pods entirely ineffective. => AVOID PODS ENTIRELY.
- External links in post body: REAL ~60% reach reduction (deprioritized to keep time-on-site).
- "Link in first comment" workaround: NOW MYTH — comments with external links suppressed up to ~80% as of early 2026. Best practice: put link only if someone asks, or use a "comment 'X' and I'll DM you" approach (but note: that itself can read as engagement-bait — use sparingly/honestly).

## ON-BRAND
Documents/carousels = ideal for an anti-hype expert: dense, save-worthy frameworks and breakdowns, currently underused (4.88%). Avoid pods absolutely. Don't over-index on short video. Newsletters bypass the feed = great owned-audience play.
