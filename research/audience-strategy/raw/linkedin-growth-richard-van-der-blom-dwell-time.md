## Verbatim claims

Overall decline (van der Blom Algorithm Insights 2025): Views -50%, engagement -25%, follower growth -59% vs prior year.

Dwell time central to ranking:
- Posts holding attention 60+ seconds see engagement rates ~15.6%
- Posts generating under 3 seconds of attention: ~1.2% engagement
- Algorithm rewards dwell time and meaningful COMMENTS, not just likes/reactions.

Content recommendations: "High-performing posts are concise, story-driven and built for SAVES, not virality." Document/PDF carousels at 6.60% engagement = highest format.

Early engagement window: "The first 30-60 minutes after posting determine the post's reach trajectory — getting strong engagement in this window signals the algorithm to proceed to stage 3" (three-stage distribution: initial test audience -> quality scoring -> broader distribution).

## ON-BRAND takeaway
Dwell time + saves + meaningful comments being the real currency is GOOD for an anti-hype expert: deep, useful, save-worthy content (frameworks, breakdowns) is exactly what wins. You don't need clickbait; you need content people actually read and save.
