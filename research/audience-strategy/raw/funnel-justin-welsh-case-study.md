# Justin Welsh — Audience-to-Revenue Case Study (primary practitioner)

Access date: 2026-06-19

## SOURCE: Growth In Reverse — "How Justin Welsh Built a $1.7M Solo Business in Just 3.5 Years"
URL: https://growthinreverse.com/justin-welsh/
Accessed: 2026-06-19

Platform growth:
- LinkedIn: 360k followers (writing daily since 2018)
- Twitter: 315k followers (started Oct 2021, reached 300k in 16 months)
- Newsletter (The Saturday Solopreneur): 77,000 subscribers (launched Jan 2022, 12 months to that)

Revenue streams (~$1.7M annually):
- Courses & digital products: $1.3M (76%). Three offerings: LinkedIn OS, Content OS, Creator MBA. ~$150 each. ~23+ sales/day (~8,667/yr).
- Newsletter sponsorships: $156k. $1,500/issue, 2 sponsors weekly = $3,000/week.
- Subscriptions: ~$108k. Monthly templates $9/mo, ~1,000 active subs.
- Affiliate: $25k.
- Coaching: remaining; minimal promotion, being phased out due to time.

Content system (10 steps):
1. Idea capture (2x weekly, 30-min sessions, 5-7 ideas each)
2. Research
3. Newsletter template: Problem -> Common solution -> Why it fails -> Alternative approach -> 4-5 tips
4. Editing (5th-grade reading level, remove tangents, add visuals)
5. Pre-newsletter teaser (Friday before)
6. Post-launch promotion (day after)
7. Twitter thread adaptation
8. LinkedIn carousel conversion
9. 6-8 more short-form pieces (listicles, stories, contrarian takes, analysis, comparisons)
10. Publish across 4-6 week stagger

KEY: One newsletter -> 10-20 social pieces, reused over weeks (not one-time posting).

Operating philosophy:
- 80/20: focus on 20% driving results; eliminate-simplify-automate-delegate the rest.
- Values own time at $1,000/hr; invests $650/mo in tools.
- VA 20 hrs/week handles customer support.

Differentiation:
- Reverse-engineered successful creators' EARLY-stage content (not current), esp. Sahil Bloom's early Twitter, to find replicable templates.
- Repurposing at scale: reuse templates monthly with updated metrics; cross-post LI->Twitter with platform-specific wording; recycle top posts after 12+ months.
- Consistency over novelty.

## SOURCE: Medium / CreatorEconomy / Substack synthesis on Welsh's path from consulting -> products
URLs: https://medium.com/@snehatyagi18/... ; https://creatoreconomy.so/p/how-i-built-an-8m-solo-business-justin-welsh ; https://substack.com/home/post/p-117771324
Accessed: 2026-06-19

Path (ON-BRAND lesson: consulting FIRST, products later):
- Left corporate 2019 after health reset. Had scaled two healthcare SaaS firms to billion-$ valuations by building sales teams.
- 2020-2021: LinkedIn audience compounded via daily text-post discipline + unusual willingness to publish operating numbers publicly.
- Began CONSULTING with SaaS companies needing scaling help; got those clients by posting on LinkedIn.
- First digital course 2020 "The LinkedIn Playbook": ~1,500 copies @ $50 = ~$75K over 15 months.
- Rebuilt as "The LinkedIn Operating System", 3x-4x price increase. 100% of LinkedIn content focused on audience growth + product directly tied to that content. V2 grossed $186k in 3 months.
- Private creator community @ $199/yr annual membership.

TAKEAWAY: content topic and offer must be the SAME subject. Audience built on topic X buys product/service about topic X. Sold consulting via free public content before/with productizing.
